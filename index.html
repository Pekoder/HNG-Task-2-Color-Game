How to play

  1. Look at the color displayed in the master box at the top
  2. Find its exact match among the six color options below
  3. Click on the option you think matches the target color
  4. Score points for correct guesses and try to beat your high score!

Scoring system

  1. Correct guess: +5 points
  2. Wrong guess: -2 points
